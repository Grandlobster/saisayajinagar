Society Redevelopment Updates

Welcome to our society's official YouTube channel and blog. Here, we regularly share essential updates for housing societies undergoing or planning for redevelopment.

Redevelopment is a complex and highly regulated process, and the role of a Project Management Consultant (PMC) is critical in ensuring its success. In this video, we cover how our society evaluated and appointed Merit Housing Redevelopment Consultants as our official PMC.

The selection process was conducted transparently and strictly followed the legal framework and guidelines provided by the Cooperative Department, Government of India. Merit Housing was chosen for their deep experience, technical knowledge, and community-first approach.

To honor this significant milestone, we were delighted to have our local corporator, Mr. Baba Dhumal, join us and felicitate the Merit Housing team. His presence and encouragement have boosted our confidence in this journey.

The video gives you a complete walkthrough of why PMC selection is so important, how to approach it, and what qualities to look for in a consultant. It’s a must-watch for every society member looking to ensure a smooth and legally compliant redevelopment.
